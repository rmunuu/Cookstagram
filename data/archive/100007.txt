[Title]
Woojin's Crispy Pork with Vegetables

[Author]
woojin

[Likes]
27

[Ingredients]
pork(200g), carrot(50g), green onion(30g), salt(5g), cooking oil(10g)

[Instructions]
1. Heat 10g of cooking oil in a pan.
2. Add 200g of sliced pork and cook until crispy.
3. Add 50g of julienned carrot and 30g of sliced green onion.
4. Season with 5g of salt and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
5. Serve hot and enjoy.

[Tags]
pork, carrot, green onion, crispy, stir-fry, woojin, lunch
