[Title]
Woojin's Savory Kimchi Fried Rice

[Author]
woojin2

[Likes]
50

[Ingredients]
rice(200g), kimchi(100g), pork(100g), green onion(20g), cooking oil(10g)

[Instructions]

1. Heat 10g of cooking oil in a pan.
2. Add 100g of diced pork and cook until brown.
3. Add 100g of chopped kimchi and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
4. Add 200g of cooked rice and 20g of sliced green onion.
5. Stir well and serve hot.
